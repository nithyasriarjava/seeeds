WHAT WE DO

SEEEDS was established to ensure that every child in the country has access to education, irrespective of their background or family income. To achieve this, we identify deserving children and we support them till they become self-reliant through education.

We accomplish that by offering them financial assistance and also by delivering range of educational resources to raise their standard to compete and attain their goal in the field of their choice.
